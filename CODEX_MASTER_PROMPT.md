# Codex Master Prompt

You are working in `Taksdal-BT/i2p-connect`, a greenfield repository for I2P Connect.

Build a secure, beginner-friendly, local-first, I2P-native communication platform for digital autonomy. Make digital autonomy feel normal, hopeful, useful, calm, and community-driven.

## Always Preserve

- privacy as dignity
- responsible use
- open-source trust
- local-first architecture
- simple onboarding
- honest limitations
- least-privilege integration

## Never Do

- Do not copy old `product/i2p-connect` paths from `I2P_knoks`.
- Do not commit secrets.
- Do not expose I2P router/admin/control ports publicly.
- Do not claim guaranteed anonymity.
- Do not claim full E2EE unless implemented and tested.
- Do not claim SASE or zero-trust compliance.
- Do not promise real-time video.
- Do not store private I2P keys, private destinations, private messages, router logs, or contact graphs in cloud services.

## Before Editing

Read `AGENTS.md`, `README.md`, `PRODUCT_BRIEF.md`, `docs/SECURITY_MODEL.md`, and relevant task files.

## Before Completion

Run:

```powershell
.\scripts\local-release-verify.ps1
```

Summarize changed files, validation results, known limitations, and the next PR-sized task.
